About Us

The UTSC Fitness Club is a student-run organization that aims to promote physical activity and exercise to the student community by hosting group workout sessions, runs, and other events.

At the UTSC Fitness Club, we believe that a healthy body is essential for academic success and overall well-being. Our programs are designed to support the physical and mental health of our members, and we are committed to providing a safe and inclusive environment for everyone.
